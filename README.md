## <span style="color: #ffcb05;">🎯 Overview</span>

**<span style="color: #8b4b5c;">R</span><span style="color: #8b4b5c;">G</span><span style="color: #8b4b5c;">X</span> <span style="color: #3598db;">| </span> <span style="color: #ffcb05;">P</span><span style="color: #3b4cca;">okemon </span><span style="color: #ff0000;">L</span><span style="color: #3b4cca;">evel-</span><span style="color: #2a9d3f;">U</span><span style="color: #3b4cca;">p!</span>** <span style="color: #e67e23;">brings a Pokemon-inspired level-up jingle into</span> <span style="color: #06c;">World of Warcraft</span><span style="color: #e67e23;">, replacing the default sound with a nostalgic monster-catching vibe across supported WoW versions.</span>

## <span style="color: #ffcb05;">✨ Features</span>

<span style="color: #2dc26b;">🎵 <strong>Pokemon Sound</strong></span> <span style="color: #3598db;">-</span> <span style="color: #e67e23;">Plays a Pokemon-inspired level-up chime every time you gain a level.</span>

<span style="color: #ffcb05;">🎚️ <strong>Quality Options</strong></span> <span style="color: #3598db;">-</span> <span style="color: #e67e23;">Choose between</span> <span style="color: #4ecdc4;">High</span><span style="color: #e67e23;">,</span> <span style="color: #4ecdc4;">Medium</span><span style="color: #e67e23;">, and</span> <span style="color: #4ecdc4;">Low</span> <span style="color: #e67e23;">sound variants.</span>

<span style="color: #ff6b6b;">🔇 <strong>Smart Muting</strong></span> <span style="color: #3598db;">-</span> <span style="color: #e67e23;">Automatically mutes WoW's default level-up sound while PLU is enabled.</span>

<span style="color: #b96ad9;">💾 <strong>Saved Settings</strong></span> <span style="color: #3598db;">-</span> <span style="color: #e67e23;">Your settings persist across sessions.</span>

<span style="color: #4ecdc4;">💬 <strong>Slash Commands</strong></span> <span style="color: #3598db;">-</span> <span style="color: #e67e23;">Control the addon with simple</span> <span style="color: #4ecdc4;">/plu</span> <span style="color: #e67e23;">commands.</span>

<span style="color: #2dc26b;">⚡ <strong>Lightweight</strong></span> <span style="color: #3598db;">-</span> <span style="color: #e67e23;">Small, efficient addon structure based on the same framework used by SRLU and FFLU.</span>

<span style="color: #ff6b6b;">🎮 <strong>Multi-Version</strong></span> <span style="color: #3598db;">-</span> <span style="color: #e67e23;">Supports Retail, Wrath, Burning Crusade, and Classic Era builds included in this repo.</span>

***

## <span style="color: #ffcb05;">📋 Slash Commands</span>

<span style="color: #e67e23;">The addon can be managed using the following slash commands</span><span style="color: #3598db;">:</span>

<div style="background-color: #0d1117; color: #58a6ff; font-family: 'Consolas', 'Monaco', monospace; border: 1px solid #30363d;">/plu help     # Show all available commands<br>/plu test     # Play test sound<br>/plu enable   # Enable the addon<br>/plu disable  # Disable the addon<br>/plu high     # Use high quality sound<br>/plu med      # Use medium quality sound (default)<br>/plu low      # Use low quality sound</div>
